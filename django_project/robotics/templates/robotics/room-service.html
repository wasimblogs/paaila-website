About Our Room Service Robot


Now, you don’t have to worry about the delivery services to your guests. Deploy Ferry at the service and bring convenience to your workers and delight your guests.



How does it work?

It’s easy to get Ferry on operation. After a guest calls for a room-delivery of toothbrush, towel, water bottles, breakfast or such, all you have to do is place the order in the enclosed compartment. Enter the room number for delivery and tap GO. Ferry will then use its inbuilt technology to navigate its way around to complete the delivery. It will even call the elevator wirelessly and alert the guest after it arrives outside their room.
